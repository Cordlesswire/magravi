News & offers











You are the authors of this fairy tale

Our family offers you hospitality and welcome, offering a complex of trulli dating back to 1933, with all the comforts needed for a real vacation.


In Magravi there is no place for either stress or for bad mood.
In our magical city of the trulli, sweet and pleasant atmosphere causes feelings of well-being and immediate relax.
The environment is typical of Puglia, surrounded by olive groves, vineyards and cherry trees, characterized by the suggestiveness of a landscape that is the mark of our territory. At B&B Magravì you can breathe in the scent of nature and tradition.

Visit a trullo























Trullo Melograno (2-3 people)

The Melograno Suite is a solution for who prefer a Simple and Sophisticated Style design, ideal for couples or small families. We offer a cozy mini-suite composed of One Master bedroom, Living/Dining room with equipped kitchen and private bathroom whit shower. Also during the winter seasons we offer our guests the opportunity to warm up by the Fireplace, in order to make pleasant and picturesque winter stays.

























Trullo Ciliegio (2 people)

The Ciliegio Suite is suitable for who want a Sophisticated and Bright Style. It consists of a one large Romantic bedroom, full equipped kitchen and comfortable private bathroom with shower.

























Trullo Ulivo (2-4 people)

The Olivo Suite is the best solution for families being the most spacious of our mini-apartments. Includes 2 Separate Bedrooms (double or single), private bathroom with shower, and a Large kitchen fully equipped with everything you need to try the preparation of the typical dishes of Apulia.















Who we are

The B&B is run entirely by our family, we are Maria, Graziana and Vitania and the combination of our names is the name of the structure, this choice is given to emphasize the character of family hospitality that we offer. The property dates back to 1933,and has been restored and made accessible thanks to the restlessness and dedicated work, which began eleven years ago by Franco the head of the family and the author of all the works of renovation.
